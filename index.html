Honest answers

The questions that keep you up at night — answered honestly

"What if the builder takes my money and disappears?"

We only recommend MahaRERA registered projects. By law, 70% of buyer funds must sit in escrow — accessible only for that project's construction. We show you how to verify this yourself before you pay anything.

"What happens to my EMI if I lose my job?"

Home Loan Protection Insurance covers your EMI in case of job loss or disability. Banks also offer moratorium options. We walk you through every protection before you sign the loan agreement.

"Is this really the right time, or will prices fall after I buy?"

We give you the infrastructure data, appreciation history and rental yields — and tell you honestly which areas are still pre-boom and which are already overpriced. Then you decide.

"What if I need to sell later — will I find a buyer?"

Navi Mumbai has strong resale liquidity near transit and airport corridors. We also walk you through Section 54 — the legal route to sell property and pay zero capital gains tax.
